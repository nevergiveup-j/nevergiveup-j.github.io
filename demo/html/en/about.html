ABOUT MANCHESTER CITY FC
Manchester City founded in 1894 are an English football team currently playing in the English Premier League.

The last five years have seen Manchester City claim all of English football’s major domestic trophies, as well as establishing them as a major force on the European stage.

Manchester City have some of the greatest players in the world who combine together to create beautiful football, these players include; Sergio ‘Kun Aguero’, Joe Hart, David Silva and Kevin De Bruyne President Xi Jinping, visited the City Football Academy on October 23rd, home to Manchester City Football Club and headquarters of its global football organisation, City Football Group.

Manchester City FC is part of City Football Group – a global network of football clubs, including New York City FC, Melbourne City FC and Yokohama F Marinos

Sergio Aguero wrote his name into Manchester City’s history books on 13 May 2012 when he scored in the last minute of the last match of the season, grabbing the winner against QPR to win the Premier League title in the most dramatic way imaginable.
The Argentine striker looked at home in English football as soon as he moved to City from Atletico Madrid in 2011. In April 2016 he scored his 100th Premier League goal for the club, at 147 games the second-fastest player to reach that mark. He won the coveted Golden Boot in 2015 for topping the Premier League scoring charts for that season.
